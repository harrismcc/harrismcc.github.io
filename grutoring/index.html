Setting up Anaconda in PATH (Windows)

  1. Use the search bar to open "View Advanced System Settings"
  2. At the bottom of the window, click "Enviornment Variables"
  3. There will be two boxes with text. In the one labeled: System Variables click on the item called "Path" (Note: This is different than PATH)
  4. Click "edit" and in the window that pops up click "add"
  5. In the text field, enter "C//Users//Username//Anaconda3//Scripts" Where "Username" is replaced with your username
  6. Finally, click ok on all open windows and restart VSCode and/or CMD (if open)
